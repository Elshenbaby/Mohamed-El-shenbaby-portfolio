About Me

Built from scratch.
Intentionally.

I'm a Faculty of Engineering student at Tanta University, majoring in Artificial Intelligence — but my story started earlier, as a logo designer before college, learning that every great system needs a human face.

From volunteering in AIESEC to leading national PR for an 800+ delegate event, from co-founding Sarmady Events (Tanta's first board games event) to building full-stack analytics platforms used by 19 branches — I operate where tech meets real-world impact.

I completed the DEPI Data Analytics Track (Ministry of Communications, Egypt), building DataWallet: a full BI-integrated financial tracking platform. Today as Country Brand Experience Director, I coach 19 LCs, generate 50k+ signups offline, and build the data infrastructure that powers national decisions.

Goal: go deeper into AI engineering, data systems, and product development while bringing the business intuition I've built from years of real-world leadership.
